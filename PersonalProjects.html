FitLife Gym

A static fitness website prototype designed in Figma to promote FitLife Gym’s workout programs, membership plans, and healthy lifestyle services. The design emphasizes a modern and energetic layout with a strong visual hierarchy, responsive-ready structure, and clear call-to-actions to engage fitness and potential members.

Trident Vintage Wear

A static fashion website prototype designed in Figma to promote Trindent's bold and timeless vintage wear collection. The layout combines high-impact visuals with retro-inspired colors and clean modern UI elements. Crafted to reflect the brand's strong identity, the design uses dynamic composition, responsive structure

MIX Real Estate

A static real estate website prototype designed in Figma to showcase MIX's collection of handcrafted luxury homes. The design emphasizes and sophistication through minimal layouts, neutral color schemes, and strong visual hierarchy. With strategically placed call-to-actions and a focus on elegant property presentation.

Farma

A static agricultural website prototype designed in Figma to promote Farma's local farmer's market initiative. The design blends organic color palettes, clean typography, and warm imagery to highlight sustainable farming practices and locally sourced products. Focused on accessibility and authenticity the layout guides.
