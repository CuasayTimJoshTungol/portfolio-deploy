About

I'm a passionate UI/UX Design Enthusiast with hands-on experience in creating responsive and user-friendly interfaces. I specialize in translating ideas into polished designs through wireframing, prototyping, and high-fidelity mockups using Figma. My work emphasizes clean design principles, usability, and accessibility to deliver seamless user experiences. In addition to design, I also build and customize WordPress websites, ensuring they're easy to manage and aligned with client goals. While I'm continuously learning and evolving, my core focus remains on intuitive design, system consistency, and modern design workflows. Sometimes, I also develop my projects using code bringing my designs to life with HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, and Tailwind CSS.

Hobby

When I'm done working on a design layout and I'm not developing it, my time is focused on learning more advanced tools that I can use in my future job, and of course I go back to coding and learning in-demand frameworks or languages.

Additionally, I enjoy gaming as a way to unwind it keeps my reflexes sharp and my mind active, making it the perfect recharge before diving back into coding and exploring new technologies.
